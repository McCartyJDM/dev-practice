About Hotel BT

Founded in 1833 by Robert Charles Winthrop shortly after completing his bar examination. He saw the need for food and lodging for delegates of the Massachusetts House of Representatives while they convened at the State House nearby in Beacon Hill. Staff at Hotel BT has entertained royalty, housed heads of State, and conducted prestigious events in our Great Hall. We take pride in providing the perfect Boston experience. Your stay at Hotel BT will be one for the memory books!
